I am a PhD student at the University of Toronto and Vector Institute, advised by Brenda Andrews and Jimmy Ba. My research is at the intersection of deep learning, computer vision and NLP. I am interested in developing algorithms that can learn meaningful representations from the real-world data, while being resistant to forgetting and inherent data noise.
